About

Hello, my name is Amanda Arpin and I created this website because I am constantly inspired by the natural beauty of the Pacific Northwest. This website is a gallery of images I have taken around Washington state that serve as design inspiration for my work. I like to incorporate my personal affinity for the Pacific Northwest into my creative designs. I am currently seeking design work, and you can find out how to add my personal flair to your page on my Contact page.

If you'd like to follow me on Twitter, my username is @amanda_arpin.
